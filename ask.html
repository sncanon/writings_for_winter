Ask

Please do not send me questions asking for advice about how to deal with rape or sexual assault.

As much as I would like to help, my own trauma prevents me from being able to answer questions of the sort, as they have the potential to severely trigger me. I wish I could help, but am unable to. I wish anyone dealing with the aftermath of rape or sexual assault all the love in the world.
