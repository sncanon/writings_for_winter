About

Meggie Royer. Female. 23 and in love with words.
"I did not expect to be me for an entire lifetime."
-Don Nace

Meggie Royer is an author and artist whose work tends to address real and contemporary issues, including suicide, abortion, rape, gender identities, and mental health.

Raised in Ames, Iowa with her triplet brother and sister, Royer holds a successful career, recieving numerous awards, publishing several books, and frequently has her work used to help others through trials.

Please use this website to read work by Meggie Royer, purchase her books, get tips or help, or send a message.
